Drawing a triangle

You can add a new shape to your design using a triangle.

You have created a new project using a preset artboard or a custom project.

You will be able to draw a basic shape in your project in Adobe XD.

You can quickly draw triangles using the Triangle tool - a drawing tool located on the left toolbar.

  1. Select the triangle tool .
  2. Drag the triangle diagonally until the it is the desired size.

As a result after following the steps above you will have drawn a triangle in Adobe XD.
