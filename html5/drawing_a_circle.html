Drawing a circle

You can add a new shape to your design using a circle.

You have created a new project using a preset artboard or a custom project.

You will be able to draw a circle in your project in Adobe XD.

You can quickly draw circles using the Ellipse tool - a drawing tool located on the left toolbar.

  1. Select the Ellipse tool .
  2. Hold down the Shift key.
  3. Drag the circle diagonally until the it is the desired size.

As a result after following the steps above you will have drawn a circle in Adobe XD.
